About me

Rasenthiran Pakeerathan

My name is Pakeerathan, and I come from a loving family. My father's name is Rasenthiran, and my mother is Mallikathevi. At the age of 17, I have already discovered my passion for code learning. My ambition is to become an aircraft engineer, as I have always been fascinated by the marvels of aviation and the intricate mechanics behind flying machines. With dedication, hard work, and a strong foundation in mathematics, I am determined to pursue my dreams and contribute to the field of aircraft engineering.
